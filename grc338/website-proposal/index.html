Target audience

Though tea is consumed by a range of ages, as it is the second most drank drink (the first being water) the primary target audience would be around the range of 25-35 year olds, though it is quite flexible within the adult age range. Additional research shows that 87% of Millenials buy and consume teas, which lines up with the age range provided.

Their primary task for visiting the website would be to browse and purchase the types of teas offered at the company, which offer a range of flavors, health benefits and scents. Outside from general consumption, tea is an additional supply found in regular crafts, which could also draw home and wellness enthusiasts,looking to find scented and natural products to take home.

About Us

Spill the Tea is an independent Specialty Tea shop, based on the Coast of California. Our name comes from the phrase used for talking, sharing information, and enjoying the story-telling and riveting nature of communicating with friends. We offer an expansive collection of tea curated by the owners and employees of the company to ensure that you, as the customer, get the best possible quality we can provide. We encourage you to browse our wares online, and if you can't wait to get your hands on our tea, drop in to our brick and morter store to experience the scents and flavors in person!
